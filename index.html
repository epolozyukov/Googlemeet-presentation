Opening question

What does a project manager actually do?

Traditional answers are familiar: meetings, reporting, coordination, progress tracking, and documentation.

What happens when AI starts doing part of this work better and faster?

PM work is already changing

PM in 2022 vs PM in 2026

2022

Manage the information flow

Collect updates, reconcile status, chase answers, prepare reports.

2026

Orchestrate intelligent systems

Shape context, validate decisions, align humans and AI-assisted workflows.

The role is shifting from managing information flow to orchestrating intelligent systems.

AI as a learning multiplier

Learning changes first

The important change is not the tool itself. It is the new way of interacting with information.

The old problem

Too much information, fragmented documentation, slow onboarding, context switching, passive learning.

The new model

AI can synthesize sources, generate briefings, explain concepts interactively, and answer questions based on your materials.

NotebookLM and similar tools

From searching information to interacting with information

Source-grounded Q&A

Ask questions only about uploaded PDFs, docs, slides, and notes.

Reusable outputs

Summaries, briefing documents, FAQs, study guides, and timelines.

New formats

Static materials can become conversational explanations or audio-style discussions.

The interface to knowledge is becoming conversational.

Rise of orchestration roles

People coordinating humans and AI systems become more valuable.

Agentic development

From copilots to AI agents

Copilot model

AI assists step by step

Writing, summarizing, generating code, answering questions.

Agentic model

AI executes larger workflows

Research, implementation, testing, debugging, documentation, coordination.

We are moving from AI as a passive tool toward AI as a semi-autonomous collaborator.
